Tech Quiz

Put tech knowledge to the test and compete for the top spot in our challenge!

Rules

● There will be a total of 3 rounds.
● Number of questions in each round:
● Round 1: 15 Questions.
● Round 2: 10 Questions.
● Round 3: 2 Questions for Each team Qualifying to Final Round.
● Timing for each question: 30 seconds
● Teams who are able to decode the clue first will go to successive rounds.
● Round 1: The first half leading contestants will qualify for the second round (1:2 ratio)
● Round 2: One-third of the players will qualify for the third round (1:3 ratio)
● Round 3: The battle between the last two teams and the winner will be decided.
● Jury decision will be the final decision.
● Exciting prizes will be awarded during the valedictory ceremony.
